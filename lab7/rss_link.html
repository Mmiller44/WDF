Argo

Argo is a film directed by Ben Afleck. It won Best Picture in the 2013 Oscars. This movie is based off of a CIA agent named Tony Mendez. Mendez specialized in extracting people from dangerous situations. In the movie, Mendez flies to Iran to rescue 5 Americans who were being threatened by the Iranian population. I found the movie to be very entertaining and suspenseful. It is not completely accurate in certain depictions, but Afleck did a great job at recreating actual scenes. While the credits roll, you can see actual footage from the events that took place.
